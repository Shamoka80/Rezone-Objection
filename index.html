Formal Objection – Full Text

To: Charleston County Council · Zoning & Planning Department

Re: Formal Opposition to Rezoning Application ZREZ-01-26-00171

I am writing to formally oppose the proposed rezoning of TMS #475-02-00-031 from Low Density Residential (R-4) to Neighborhood Commercial (NC).

1. Incompatibility with Existing Land Use

The subject property is situated within an established residential area characterized by low-density housing, a place of worship, and a daycare facility. The introduction of Neighborhood Commercial zoning is inconsistent with the existing land use pattern and would disrupt the residential integrity of the area.

2. Proximity to Sensitive Community Uses

The property is located in close proximity to a church and an active daycare facility. The introduction of commercial activity — particularly one associated with smoking-related products — raises significant concerns regarding exposure, appropriateness, and overall compatibility with environments serving children and families.

3. Public Health and Welfare Concerns

The proposed rezoning introduces the potential for uses that may adversely impact public health and community well-being. Given the presence of children and families in the immediate vicinity, such uses are not aligned with the best interests of the surrounding community.

4. Increased Traffic and Operational Impact

Rezoning to Neighborhood Commercial would likely result in increased traffic, parking demand, and extended operating hours. The current infrastructure and residential street design are not suited to accommodate this level of commercial activity.

5. Spot Zoning Consideration

The requested rezoning appears to create an isolated commercial designation within a predominantly residential area. This raises concerns of spot zoning, which is inconsistent with sound planning principles and may not align with the County's comprehensive land use framework.

Conclusion

For the reasons outlined above, I respectfully request that Charleston County Council deny the rezoning application ZREZ-01-26-00171 in order to preserve the character, safety, and well-being of the surrounding community.
